New conditions for immigration

You probably noticed that in 2016 immigration reform was a

hot topic in politics. What we are seeing now is the

implementation new rules that Stortinget passed in the

summer and autumn of 2016.

In short, they came up with some additional tasks to

complete before you can receive PR or citizenship. Another

new rule is that the language requirements must now be met

also by older immigrants (up to 67 years of age).
